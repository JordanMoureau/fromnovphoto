I'm Jordan *waves

There is one thing that every photographer has in common: We all love love. It’s that golden glow, the look in his eyes, the tiny arms and the cherub cheeks, the way we treasure eachother memorialized forever in a photograph. Wow, I’m making myself choke up right now. See what I mean!

On about me though: I grew up on a little hobby farm in north eastern Georgia where the humidity hits you like a brick wall if you try to leave the A/C. It never managed to stop me running around the woods like a little heathen though.

Since then I’ve lived that gypsy life. Migrating from east coast to west coast whenever the mood suited. It fit my wild heart well, but lately roots have been calling and I must answer.

At the moment I have gorgeous little third floor apartment in Liberty Lake Washington that I share with my three kids, Jack, Danny and Gemma. I’ve put my heart and soul into making it a home and I think I’ll stay a while :)
